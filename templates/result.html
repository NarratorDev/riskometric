Why This Experiment Matters

Investors often behave very differently when facing gains versus losses. Many people: become conservative while earning profits, but become aggressive when trying to avoid losses.

This psychological behavior influences:

  • Stock market crashes
  • Panic selling during volatility
  • Holding losing stocks too long
  • Emotional portfolio decisions
  • Overtrading and excessive risk-taking
