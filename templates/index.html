Riskometric Assessment

Welcome to the Behavioral Finance Assessment Platform developed for the Center of Investments and Capital Markets (CICM).

This interactive assessment evaluates how psychological biases influence financial decision-making and investment behavior.

What This Assessment Measures

  • Loss Aversion
  • Risk Appetite
  • Certainty Effect
  • Sunk Cost Fallacy
  • Herd Mentality
  • Overconfidence Bias

Why This Matters

Investor psychology strongly affects stock market behavior, portfolio management, trading decisions, and financial risk-taking. Many investment mistakes are driven not by lack of knowledge, but by behavioral biases and emotional decision-making.

Instructions

  • Answer all questions independently
  • Do not discuss answers with others
  • Select the option closest to your instinctive decision
  • Estimated completion time: 3–5 minutes
